How it works

  • Add a card with Add New Node.
  • Edit by clicking inside the message box and typing/pasting.
  • Copy with one click — you’ll see a small Copied! notice.
  • Delete removes a card (with confirmation).
  • Saved automatically in your browser (localStorage), so refreshing won’t erase your messages.
  • Reorder by dragging the ↕ handle.
  • Search filters cards as you type.
  • Dark mode toggles the theme and remembers your choice.
