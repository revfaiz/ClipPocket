How It Works

1

Add a Message Card

Click Add New Node to create a new message card. You can add as many as you need!

2

Edit Your Messages

Click inside the message box and type or paste your frequently used text. You can also add a title to organize your messages.

3

Copy with One Click

Click the Copy button on any card to copy the message to your clipboard. You'll see a "Copied!" notification.

4

Reorder Cards

Drag the ↕ handle to reorder your cards. Your order is automatically saved.

5

Search Messages

Use the search bar to quickly find specific messages. Or press Ctrl + K to focus the search.

6

Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark themes using the theme button. Your preference is remembered.

💾 Data Storage

All your messages are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. They persist even after refreshing the page. No data is sent to any server.
